Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.)

Is the card physical?

No, this is a virtual card sent to your registered email upon successful purchase.

Can others use my card?

The card is non-transferable and valid for one user only.

Is there an additional fee for benefits like CE credits or video recordings?

No, all promised benefits are included in your membership.

Can I pay registration fees for others with my card?

No, this is a membership card, not a payment card. It provides access to conferences and associated benefits for you only.

What is the card's validity period?

Lifetime validity.

How many conferences can I attend?

Gain lifetime access to conferences with unlimited participation as a Listener or Participant.

How do I claim the Speaker discount?

Email your card number and conference details to us. We’ll send you a 20% discount code.

What happens if I lose my card details?

Email us at support@researchsummits.com with your registered email stating, “I Forgot My Card Details.” Our team will assist you.
